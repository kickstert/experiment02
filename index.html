Anya Forger

Anya Forger is one of the main characters of the SPY x FAMILY series. She is the adopted daughter of Loid and Yor Forger and is a telepath. She attends Eden Academy as a first-year student in Cecile Hall, Class 3.

Loid Forger

is one of the main characters of the SPY x FAMILY series. His true name is currently unknown, as he has discarded everything about his past to work for his home country, Westalis, as an undercover spy who acts under the codename "Twilight."

Yor Forger

is one of the main characters of the SPY x FAMILY series. While she works as an ordinary office clerk at Berlint City Hall, she also leads a secret life as a Garden assassin under the code name "Thorn Princess."
